Privacy Policy

MediaSnatch was built with a strong respect for user privacy in mind. Created by Ellie the Dev, this free and open-source media downloader does not collect any personal data directly. Our mission is to provide a lightweight and privacy-conscious tool for downloading media from platforms like Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and Facebook — without compromising your digital safety.

What We Don’t Collect

  • ❌ No account required
  • ❌ No personal data storage
  • ❌ No activity tracking

Local Processing

All media links are processed locally in your browser to complete downloads. No media or URL data is stored or logged on any MediaSnatch servers.

Ads and Third-Party Services

MediaSnatch may display ads from third-party networks like Google AdSense to keep the service free. These ad providers may use cookies or device identifiers to personalize ads based on your interests or general location.

You can learn more about how Google uses data here. MediaSnatch does not directly access or store any of this data.

Cookies

While MediaSnatch itself does not set cookies, third-party ad providers may use cookies or similar technologies to serve relevant ads. These can usually be controlled via your browser settings.

Third-Party Content

Downloads are pulled from external platforms (like YouTube, Instagram, etc.). Please make sure you're following their respective terms and copyright policies.

Your Responsibility

  • Respect copyright and fair use laws.
  • Do not misuse MediaSnatch to violate platform terms.
  • Ensure lawful and ethical use of all downloaded content.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future to reflect changes in functionality or compliance. Check back occasionally for updates.
