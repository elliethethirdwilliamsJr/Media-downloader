About MediaSnatch

MediaSnatch is a free and open-source media downloader built with love by Ellie the Dev. This tool was designed to simplify your experience when downloading media from popular platforms such as Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and Facebook — all in one smooth and responsive interface.

The idea behind MediaSnatch was to create something lightweight, mobile-friendly, and intuitive that doesn't require bloated software or complicated setup. Whether you're saving videos for educational purposes, offline viewing, or creative reuse (within the limits of fair use), this tool aims to make that process easier for everyone.

Ellie the Dev is passionate about building useful tools for the internet community. With MediaSnatch, the goal was to offer a no-paywall, privacy-respecting solution to everyday users who just want simple access to the content they already love.
